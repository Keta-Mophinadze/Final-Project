Keta Mophinadze

Vinyasa Flow, Hatha, Yin

What is yoga for me?

It is the constant care of my body and mind that helps me to have a healthy and harmonious relationship with myself. Daily journey into the world of yoga is a pleasant and interesting process that excites me.

I became interested in yoga a few years ago. At first it was one of my hobbies that helped me to be physically active all the time and improve my mental state. In the process, yoga took such a big place in my life that I decided to turn my favorite hobby into my profession and became a yoga instructor. I actively started working with my friends to create a platform that would be accessible to any person, from any point on earth. My goal is to share my experience as much as possible with people interested in yoga and to further strengthen their interest in this field.

Berlin, Germany
